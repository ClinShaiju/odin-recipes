Bread Pudding

Bread pudding is a pudding whose primary ingredient is bread. It has bread in it. It is very popular. It is usually served with ice cream. There is more to be said about it, but that is unimportant.

Ingredients

  • Bread. It is generally hard to make bread pudding without bread. If you try that, you get pudding.
  • Everything has water, so make sure you have a healthy amount of H2O on hand.
  • There may be sugar. Who knows?
  • Raisins. Never make anything without raisins. Don’t even try it.
  • Milk. Milk come from cow. Milk good. Need milk for pudding.
  • Liquid Nitrogen

Steps

  1. Rip the bread. Put it in a bowl and soak it in milk and water. At this point you’ll have something between toxic sludge and diarrhea.
  2. Add sugar. Lots and lots of sugar. 80 cups minimum.
  3. Put it all into a pan with raisins. Raisins will give it that amazing texture of an old bean bag.
  4. Put it into the oven. Bake at 260 degrees C.
  5. Mix in the liquid nitrogen
  6. Enjoy!
  7. Don’t die. You may find this difficult with liquid nitrogen in your food but you’ll manage.
