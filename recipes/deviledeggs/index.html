Deviled Eggs

Deviled eggs are eggs that have been deviled. What that means we don’t know, but it doesn’t matter.

Ingredients

  • Eggs.
  • Fire. If you can’t personally ask the devil to devil your eggs, fire will suffice.
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Hard Boil your eggs. This means boil them until they are hard.
  2. Ask the devil to devil your eggs. If he refuses or if you are unable to reach him on account of there being a fire extinguisher on the premises, you could die and go ask him or you could simply light yourself on fire and wave a trident around. Then you would be able to devil the eggs. Proceed to do so.
  3. Add mustard. How you do it it doesn’t matter, but just add mustard.
  4. Enjoy.
